Nock the Rock

This course will test your stamina with a variety of terrain. It starts out with pavement, changes to gravel, then a trail through a field, next more gravel road, and finishes up the last couple miles on pavement. Each road has gentle hills, though about half of the course is flat, with the second half being the "flattest."

This is an incredibly scenic, 13.2 half marathon run in the beautiful Rocky Mountains surrounding Soda Springs, Idaho. Next race will be held June 24th, 2017 at 8:00 AM for the half-marathon. The kids 1 mile race it will start at 7:30 AM. The starting line is at 311 W 4th S.
