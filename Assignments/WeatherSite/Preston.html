Preston Night Rodeo

The 83rd annual celebration of That Famous Preston Night Rodeo begins today, July 26, 2018, and runs through Saturday, July 28. Already, Benson Park is filling with booths full of food, finds and fun. State Street's sidewalk sale has begun and PRCA merchandise is available at the rodeo grounds.

The parade, which circles from First North south on State Street, then back north again at Second South on First East to Second North, begins at 6:30 p.m. The rodeo starts at 8 p.m. Few there will be that will find tickets at this late date, although there are a few individuals seats scattered throughout the arena. The Kiwanis and Lions clubs will fire up their grills so fans can enjoy their traditional rodeo burgers. The clubs use funds raised from the sales at their food stands to support community projects.
